Online Verifiable CPD 2020 (10 hours)

Welcome to the REA Verifiable Continuing professional development Programme for 2020.

Under the new continuing professional development (CPD) programme, licensees must complete 4 hours of mandatory topics and 6 hours of elective topics. This years' elective topics should preferably be different to the topics completed in 2019.

The mandatory topics for 2020 are:

  • Ethics (2 hours)
  • Buyer Experience (2 hours)


In order to complete your Verifiable Continuing Professional Development for 2020, you must complete all four mandatory and six elective hours for $99.00 (inc. GST)

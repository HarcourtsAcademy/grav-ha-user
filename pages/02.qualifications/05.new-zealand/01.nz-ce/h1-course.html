Online Verifiable CE 2018 (10 hours Topics 1-5)

Welcome to the REAA Verifiable Continuing Education Programme for 2018.

It comprises of ten hours of material split into the following five topics:

  • Topic 1 - Supervision
  • Topic 2 - Appraisal
  • Topic 3 - Knowing the parties to the contract
  • Topic 4 - Methamphetamine standard and disclosure obligations in situations involving affected properties
  • Topic 5 - Multiple offers


All five topics are compulsory and there are no specialist strands. In order to complete your Verifiable Continuing Education for 2018, you must complete all five topics for $69.00 (inc. GST)
